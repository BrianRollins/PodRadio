PodRadio.js Demo

By Brian Rollins

A simple little demo of loading and playing podcast RSS feeds. It uses the Yahoo! YQL engine to bypass cross-domain issues.

As you can see with the demo, you can load multiple instances of players on one page. The limiting factor is the client's memory and resources. It uses the HTML5 audio player, but you can tweak it to build your own controls.
